**What you'll learn**

---

## Short summary

Evaluation requires test sets, human labels, and automated metrics. Use human-in-the-loop for high-value tasks.

---

## Key concepts

- **Test sets** — Curate representative queries and correct answers.
- **Automated metrics** — Use BLEU/ROUGE for some tasks, but prefer task-specific metrics.
- **Human review** — Essential for nuanced judgment.

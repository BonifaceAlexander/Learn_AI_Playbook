## Short summary

Decide between serverless, containerized, or hybrid deployments depending on latency, cost, and scaling needs.

---

## Key concepts

- **Serverless** — Easy scaling for stateless tasks.
- **Containers** — Good for controlled environments.
- **Hybrid** — Client-side BYOK for heavy inference

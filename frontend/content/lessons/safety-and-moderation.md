## Short summary

Safety includes content filtering, rate-limiting, and privacy-aware storage. Build guardrails before public release.

---

## Key concepts

- **Filter pipelines** — Detect and block profanity, hate, or PII.
- **Rate limits** — Protect against abuse and cost spikes.
- **Data retention** — Minimize stored user data and keys.

---

## Exercise

- Implement a simple profanity filter and test edge cases.
- Define retention policy for ephemeral keys.
